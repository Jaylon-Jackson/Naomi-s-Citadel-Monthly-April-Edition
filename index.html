Before We Begin

This reading uses four separate decks, each pulled and read on their own before they are brought into conversation. The Lenormand and Kipper map the external reality of your month — the events, the people, the conditions, what is moving and what is not. The Tarot and Oracle speak to the inner landscape: what you are being asked to embody, what needs to be released, what your soul already knows about this season.

You will recognize yourself in the Tarot and Oracle cards in ways I cannot fully predict, because those cards speak your language more than mine. I will give you the card’s identity and then what I see it doing in your reading. What it actually means in your life, you will know. That is the point.

I

The Lenormand

External Reality · The Month’s Terrain

The Witch’s Tableau · 16 Cards

  • Q1 — Recent Past · Conscious: Fish · Birds · Clover · Dog
  • Q2 — Recent Past · Hidden: Clouds · Crossroads · Moon · Fox
  • Q3 — Near Future · Conscious: Ship · Tower · Woman · Stork
  • Q4 — Near Future · Hidden: Stars · Bear · Sun · Scythe
Lenormand

Q1 — Recent Past · Conscious

Fish into Birds reads as financial conversation: money discussed, negotiated, worried over, and circulated rather than sitting in silent abundance. Clover and Dog then complete that conscious past with a brief but genuine lucky break alongside someone loyal and reliably present.

Within the quadrant, Fish + Clover suggests a real financial opening, Birds + Dog points to important conversations with a trusted person, Fish + Dog speaks to loyalty in a resource situation, and Birds + Clover softens the chatter into good or welcome news. The overall story is that Naomi already knows the recent past held money talk, a short-lived bright patch, and real support.

Q2 — Recent Past · Hidden

Clouds into Crossroads shows confusion around a decision that has been developing below the surface, while Moon adds hidden feeling, intuition, and the need for recognition that has not been fully spoken. Fox in this hidden past is the sharpest signal here: strategy, self-protection, or an agenda that has not been consciously examined.

Clouds + Moon reads as emotional confusion, Crossroads + Fox as a choice with a cunning edge beneath it, Clouds + Fox as deliberate murk or obscuring, and Moon + Crossroads as intuitive awareness of a decision before it becomes explicit. Beneath the luckier conscious past, something unresolved and strategic has been quietly shaping the ground.

Q3 — Near Future · Conscious

Ship moving into Tower says Naomi is already in motion toward something formal, structured, established, or institutionally significant. Woman and Stork facing one another make the near future especially clear: she and change are in direct dialogue, and she already knows it is approaching.

Ship + Woman reads as Naomi herself launching into movement, Tower + Stork as positive change through an established structure, Ship + Stork as a journey that carries improvement, and Tower + Woman as her movement toward more authority, containment, or formal positioning. This quadrant reads as conscious movement toward a more defined future.

Q4 — Near Future · Hidden

Stars and Sun together in the hidden future create one of the strongest positive undercurrents in the whole tableau: clarity, hope, success, and real brightness operating below the surface. Bear and Scythe complicate that promise by showing power, money, or authority tied to a decisive cut that may not be consciously anticipated.

Stars + Sun suggests extraordinary positive convergence, Bear + Scythe points to a sharp move by a powerful or financial force, Stars + Scythe becomes clarity that cuts, and Sun + Bear shows success involving authority or resources. The hidden future therefore holds both Naomi’s strongest success pattern and a severance that arrives with force.

Grid Logic

Row 1 is the visible conscious story: Fish · Birds · Ship · Tower — money talk moving toward structure. Row 2 is the intimate human experience: Clover · Dog · Woman · Stork — luck, support, Naomi herself, and positive change. Row 3 moves from confusion into guidance and authority, while Row 4 hides Moon · Fox · Sun · Scythe — feeling, strategy, success, and a decisive ending beneath awareness.

Column 3 is the spine: Ship · Woman · Stars · Sun, the most positive line in the spread and the one that runs directly through Naomi. Column 4 — Tower · Stork · Bear · Scythe — suggests a structured change that culminates in authority and a clean cut, most likely at the far edge of the month.

The main diagonal, Fish · Dog · Stars · Scythe, reads as financial loyalty moving through clarity into severance. The counter diagonal, Tower · Woman · Crossroads · Moon, shows Naomi moving through structure into a choice with deep emotional current beneath it.

II

The Kipper

Directional Anchor · People & Positions

Directional Box

  • Above: Parlour · High Honours · Rich Gent
  • Center: Siren 2
  • Below: Endings · Sorrows · Great Fortune
  • Ahead / Left: Gift
  • Behind / Right: Change

Siren 2 is Naomi’s significator and is facing right, which means what lies to her right is behind her and what lies to her left is ahead of her according to the spread guide’s emphasis on Kipper directionality. That makes High Honours the conscious aspiration above her, Gift something already received or moving away, Change the thing she is actively facing into, and Sorrows the unconscious burden that is already oriented toward Great Fortune.

The top row connects private immediacy, recognition, and a younger man; the middle row is Naomi’s own line from what she has received into what she is changing into; and the bottom row becomes the emotional spine of the reading because Endings and Sorrows both funnel toward Great Fortune. The overall box is therefore highly positive even while still acknowledging grief and closure.

Where Her Eyes Are — High Honours

High Honours is the conscious aspiration here, the elevated outcome Naomi can already sense at the level of status, recognition, or being properly acknowledged. In this spread it does not feel abstract; it feels like the visible crest she is oriented toward.

Ahead — Gift

Gift reads as something already given or moving away depending on orientation, which gives this position a subtle bittersweet quality. It still belongs to Naomi’s line, but the directional logic says it may be better understood as something received, spent, or already passing out of the center of the story.

Below — Endings · Sorrows · Great Fortune

Endings and Sorrows funnel toward Great Fortune, which is one of the clearest structural promises in the entire month. The emotional weight is real, but the spread refuses to treat it as terminal; it is pointed toward a better outcome rather than away from one.

Above — Parlour · High Honours · Rich Gent

This upper line feels social, visible, and relational. Rich Gent connects to a polished or resource-bearing masculine figure, which makes the atmosphere above Naomi feel tied to recognition, presentation, and a person who carries either resources, polish, or influence into the picture.

Behind — Change

Change shows Naomi is emerging from transformation rather than standing outside it. The people layer in this spread is directional and relational, not abstract, so this card reads less like theory and more like lived motion already in progress around her body and position.

Cross-Deck Link

The Kipper box becomes sharper when read beside the other decks: recognition above, grief below, and a polished masculine figure in the upper field all echo the month’s larger pattern of movement, caution, and consequential advancement. The people layer is therefore not separate from the Lenormand or Oracle messages; it gives them a face and a direction.

T1

Knight of Pentacles reversed reads as the shadow of grinding without reassessing: persistence that has turned into rut, stubborn material effort, and the temptation to answer imbalance by doubling down on the same method.

Together these cards do not condemn Naomi’s goals; they critique the operating method. The message is that the month asks her to release the habit of trying to muscle through misalignment by working harder and forcing equilibrium faster.

T2

Temperance reversed intensifies that message by showing forced balance, overcorrection, impatience, and a healing process being pushed past its real rhythm.

O1

The Astronomer as Lesson says the map for this month is not primarily practical or historical; it comes through intuition, discovery, and a willingness to look beyond old frameworks.

O2

The Patron reversed as Guidance warns that a mentor-like or polished figure may not be giving as much as he appears to promise. Because the guide explicitly notes that Guidance often connects to a Kipper person or Lenormand event, the cross-link to Rich Gent and Tower + Fox becomes especially potent here.

O3

The Waker reversed as Undercurrent reframes everything: too much external focus, neglected interior signals, and the need to ask honestly how Naomi feels and what those feelings are sourced from.

Convergence

The month wants to hand Naomi something brighter than her current operating method knows how to receive.

IV

The Full Picture

All Four Layers Together

What the External Map Says

Externally, the reading is remarkably strong: High Honours above the significator, Great Fortune closing the Kipper, and Stars with Sun hidden in the future all point toward real advancement, clarity, and positive outcome.

What the Inner Map Says

Internally, the cards show a mismatch between April’s opportunity and Naomi’s current method. The shadow cards say stop grinding and stop forcing balance, the Astronomer says intuition must outrank old logic, the Patron reversed warns against overvaluing a persuasive guide, and the Waker reversed insists the real blind spot is inside rather than outside.

Relational Caution

Rich Gent + Patron reversed + Tower/Fox forms the reading’s clearest relational caution: a younger or polished masculine figure tied to structure, strategy, and uneven exchange.

The Corridor

At the same time, Sorrows facing Great Fortune and the Lenormand’s Stars + Sun say that pain is not the final state here — it is the corridor toward a better outcome.

The Necessary Cut

The Scythe and Endings are not treated as random destruction in this reading; they look more like the necessary cut that makes the positive future fully available. What ends near the close of April may feel sharp, but the deeper pattern suggests it clears the path for the very fortune, recognition, and change the rest of the spread has already been promising.

Naomi’s month is not blocked. It is moving. But it asks her to stop forcing clarity from the outside and start listening for what her inner life already knows.

A Note to You

Naomi,

This month does not read as empty or stagnant. It reads as active, structured, and consequential. There are real signs of advancement here, but the reading is equally clear that movement is not the same thing as alignment.

You are not being asked to do more of the same just because you want the outcome badly. The cards suggest that pushing harder from the same internal posture will not make the month open faster. What opens it is honesty. Honesty about what you feel, honesty about where you are tired, honesty about who may be promising more than they can truly give, and honesty about what has already ended even if part of you is still standing in its echo.

The beautiful thing in this spread is that the future is not weak. It is strong. High Honours, Great Fortune, Stars, and Sun are not minor reassurances. They are major signals. But they do not remove the need for the cut. They tell you the cut is survivable and meaningful.

If something falls away this month, do not read that as proof you were abandoned by the path. Read it as the path clarifying itself.

— J
